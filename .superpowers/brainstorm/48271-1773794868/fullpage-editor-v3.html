Common Fire Hazards in the Workplace
Text
Understanding where fires start is the first step in preventing them. The most common causes in workplaces are electrical faults, improper storage of flammable materials, and human error such as leaving heat sources unattended.
List
  • Overloaded or faulty electrical sockets and extension leads
  • Flammable liquids stored near ignition sources
  • Blocked or obstructed fire exits and evacuation routes
  • Unattended cooking equipment or hot surfaces
