What it does

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Block-based editing

Compose lessons from headings, text, images, video, quizzes, accordions, and more — no code needed.

🔗
Instant publish

One click to generate a shareable URL. Embed it on Google Sites or link from anywhere.

SCORM export

Export a SCORM 1.2 package and drop it straight into any LMS — Moodle, Blackboard, you name it.

Courses look great on desktop, tablet, and phone without any extra effort.

Sign in and your courses follow you. Works offline too — saves to localStorage when you're not logged in.

Rich text editing

Full formatting support — bold, italic, links, lists — wherever text appears in your course.
