## Design Context

### Users
Course authors (educators, instructional designers, L&D professionals) who build e-learning content and learners who consume it. Authors already have AI workflows — TideLearn is the structural layer. Learners experience courses through the viewer, often in focused reading sessions.

### Brand Personality
**Calm, clear, professional.** TideLearn reduces cognitive load — the interface stays out of the way so content shines. No whimsy, no gimmicks. Trustworthy and precise like a well-made instrument.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Clean, light, spacious. Ocean/teal palette evokes depth without heaviness.
- **References**: Notion's reading clarity, Linear's UI precision, Stripe Docs' typography.
- **Anti-references**: Canva's visual noise, generic LMS dashboards, anything with "generate with AI" buttons.
- **Theme**: Light only. No dark mode.

### Design Principles
1. **Content is king** — The viewer is a reading environment first. Minimize chrome, maximize reading area. Typography and whitespace do the heavy lifting.
2. **Quiet confidence** — Use the teal palette sparingly for wayfinding (progress, active states, CTAs). The ocean palette should feel like an accent, not a flood.
3. **Accessible by default** — WCAG AA minimum. Semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, proper ARIA. No decorative elements that compromise usability.
4. **System consistency** — Use Rockpool design tokens (CSS vars), Tailwind utilities, and shadcn/ui components. Never hard-code values that exist as tokens. `font-sans` = DM Sans, `font-display` = Lora.
5. **Progressive disclosure** — Show what's needed, when it's needed. Sidebar for navigation, bottom bar for pagination, progress bar for motivation. Each element earns its space.

### Design Tokens (Quick Reference)
- **Fonts**: `font-sans` (DM Sans), `font-display` (Lora)
- **Layout**: `--topbar-h: 48px`, `--sidebar-w-viewer: 200px`, `--reading-max-w: 680px`, `--content-px: 64px`
- **Colors**: `--teal-bright: #14b8a6`, `--teal-primary: #0d9488`, `--text-muted: #64748b`, `--border-subtle: #e0fdf4`, `--ocean-surface: #0f1f1d`
- **Surfaces**: `--surface: #fff`, `--surface-subtle: #f8fffe`, `--surface-tint: #f0fdfb`
- **Radius**: `--radius-sm: 6px`, `--radius-md: 8px`
